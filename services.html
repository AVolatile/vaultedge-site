Common Questions

What teams usually ask before kickoff

Do you only work on AI projects?
No. AI is one tool in the stack. We also fix intake systems, delivery operations, reporting, portals, and web workflows that have nothing to do with AI.
Can you work alongside our internal team?
Yes. Many engagements are collaborative. We often plug into an existing operator, PM, or technical lead rather than replacing them.
What does a first engagement typically look like?
Most projects begin with a discovery sprint followed by a scoped implementation plan. That keeps the work grounded in actual constraints before build time starts.
Do you support post-launch optimization?
Yes. We usually include a stabilization period to tune the system after the first live cycles and hand off the operating rhythm cleanly.
