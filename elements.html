03 FAQ

What happens in a discovery sprint?
We map the workflow, identify risk and ownership gaps, and turn ambiguity into a sequence of buildable decisions.
How quickly can work begin?
Most engagements start within seven days of scope alignment, provided the right owners are available for kickoff.
Do you require a specific tool stack?
No. We adapt to the existing stack first and only recommend tool changes when the operating model actually needs them.
What deliverables do teams receive?
Typical outputs include roadmaps, workflow diagrams, operating rules, implementation notes, and post-launch ownership guidance.
Can you support a partial rollout?
Yes. We often stage delivery so one workflow or one client-facing surface stabilizes before the next phase begins.
What if internal alignment is weak?
That becomes the first problem we solve. Weak ownership is a delivery risk, not a side issue.
